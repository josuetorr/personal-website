Biography

The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do.

- Steve Jobs

My name is Josh Torres, I am 27 years old and currently living in Montreal, Canada. I am passionate by technology, mathematics, physics and business. I studied theoretical physics at Condordia university from 2012 to 2014. I later decided to study software engineering at UQAM, and now here I am building my own website.

Ever since I learnt about computer programming, I became obsessed with the idea of building things. I found it profoundly captivating the concept of bringing your ideas to life with a couple of thousand to millions of lines of code. I believe that this platform allows me to express myself to my fullest potentiel.

Lately, I have focused on web development. I learnt nodejs for the backend in conjuction with Express.js in order to build RESTful APIs. I learnt the basics of a JavaScript framework called Vue.js. However, my passion is without a doubt low level programming. I adore coding in C and C++. I plan on building some homebrew projects that will be listed on my projects page once their Github page will be up and running.

Projects

Grocerie app

Web-scraping based application where we gather prices of grocerie products. The users can select a range of prices for which they are willing to pay for a product. They will be notified on a user selected time basis stating where they can find their selected products for their desired price.
